About Us

Human-centered AI innovation from Team RTX

Team RTX is a group of innovators passionate about AI, agentic systems, and human-AI interaction. Our mission is to build digital experiences that feel human, intelligent, and supportive. We blend research, design, and engineering to create products that understand context, emotion, and intent.

Based at SRM University, Andhra Pradesh, we work with retailers and experience designers to bring empathy into automation. KAI reflects our belief that conversational AI should be human-aligned and business-aware.
